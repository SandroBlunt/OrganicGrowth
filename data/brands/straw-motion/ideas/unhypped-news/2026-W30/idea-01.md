## Angle
The model topping a coding leaderboard right now isn't a locked-up US flagship you rent by the token —
it's Moonshot AI's Kimi K3, a 2.8-trillion-parameter open-weight model anyone can download for free.
Meanwhile Alibaba's Qwen3.8-Max is being rated "second only to Anthropic's Claude Fable 5." The tension:
the US still holds the very top spot, but the gap between "pay-to-rent frontier" and "free-to-own open
weights" just shrank to almost nothing. Break it down for a builder deciding where to spend.
