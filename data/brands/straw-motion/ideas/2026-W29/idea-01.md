## Angle

"AI that does the task, not just talks about it" in the same stretch. Break it down as news for a
business owner: what each one actually does on your behalf, where it still needs you, and why three
rivals landing this at once means the "agent" era just went mainstream. No hype — a plain-English
status report on where hands-off AI really is today.
