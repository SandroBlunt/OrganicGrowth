## Angle
Two-part news, one story: Anthropic brought back its Fable 5 model AND published a trick to get almost
all of its power for well under half the cost. The interesting part for a small operator isn't the
relaunch — it's that "top-tier AI" and "affordable" stopped being opposites. Break down the relaunch
plainly, then explain the cost move in one clear idea.

## Hook concept
Open on the counter-intuitive promise — near-flagship results for a fraction of the price — and tee up
"here's the catch, and whether it's real." Let the cost surprise carry the open. (Concept only.)

## Talking points
- What's back: Fable 5, in plain terms — flagship-tier, relaunched.
- The cost trick explained simply — the "96% of the power, under half the price" idea without the mechanics.
- Why this matters if you've been priced out of the best models.
- The honest limit: where the cheaper path costs you something (speed, effort, edge cases).
- Close on the takeaway: the best AI is getting cheaper to run — plan for it.
