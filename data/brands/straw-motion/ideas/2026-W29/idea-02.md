## Angle
OpenAI's newest model is now on for everyone — so the useful question isn't "is it powerful," it's
"does anything change for me today?" Break the release down plainly: what's genuinely better, what's
the same as last week, and who actually feels the difference. Cut through the launch-day noise instead
of adding to it — that's the whole No-Hype promise.

## Hook concept
Open on the everyone-has-it-now moment, then pivot straight to the skeptic's question — "worth the
hype or not?" — so the viewer stays for the honest verdict rather than the marketing. (Concept only.)

## Talking points
- What "live for everyone" means in practice — free vs paid, and where it shows up.
- Two or three real improvements in plain terms (not benchmark numbers).
- The honest "meh" column: where it won't feel different from what you already use.
- Who should care today (a builder vs a casual user) and who can wait.
- Close on a grounded verdict: upgrade worth trying, or hype to ignore.
